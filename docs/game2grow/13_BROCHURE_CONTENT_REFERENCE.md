## Core Identity

## Organization
Game2Grow is a Pan India training and talent development company focused on simulation-based experiential learning for students, professionals, and organizations.

## Vision
To empower students and working professionals to perform at the peak of their potential through the power of play.

## Mission
To provide gamified, simulation-based learning experiences that transform individuals into confident, capable leaders.
