# Project Scope and Requirements

## Brand
- Company: Game2Grow
- Tagline: Experience. Learn. Grow.
- Primary colors: Black `#000000`, Gold `#C9A84C`
- Mandatory brand text treatment:
  - `GAME` in white
  - `2GROW` in gold

## Target Audiences
1. Corporate HR, L&D leaders, CHROs
2. College management, principals, placement officers

## Core Goals
- Present Game2Grow as a premium gamified learning brand.
- Drive qualified leads through clear CTAs.
- Showcase outcomes and social proof.
