Organization
Game2Grow is a Pan India training and talent development company that transforms how individuals and organizations build
skills — through carefully designed game-based simulations and experiential methodologies. Founded by Anjen Fernando, a
certified Professional Trainer in Experiential Learning (IIPE, Canada), Game2Grow serves students, working professionals, and
corporates across India — improving employability, confidence, and industry readiness.

Methodology
 People learn best by doing. Game2Grow training methodology is rooted in the science of experiential learning through play.
Every session is designed as an immersive game-based experience where participants make decisions, face consequences,
reflect, and grow. Our scientifically backed techniques include:
Game-Based Simulations
Real-world scenarios recreated as strategic games and board simulations.
Role-Play & Dramatics
Live dramatization of workplace and life situations for deep insight.
Case Studies & Debriefs
Structured reflection to extract and apply learning from every game.
Catharsis & Transformation
Powerful exercises that break limiting beliefs and build new habits.
